DEVELOPED IN CHICAGO

Self-taught. Ten-plus years in the field.

Ernie is a freelance photographer, graphic designer, and website designer based in the Chicago area. No design school — just a decade of client work, late nights learning new tools, and a habit of shipping things that hold up under real use.

Day to day, that means shooting events and portraits, building brand systems and print collateral, and designing and coding the websites that carry it all. The throughline is craft: get the exposure right, get the type right, get the code right.

  • Photography — events, portrait, product
  • Graphic design — brand identity, print, menus & collateral
  • Web design — design and front-end build
